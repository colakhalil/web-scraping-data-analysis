MFA Lecture Series on “Compassionate Leadership”

June 15, 2022


As a part of the MFA lecture series, the Ministry invited Dr. Will Parks, Resident Representative, UNICEF, to speak on Compassionate Leadership to the officials of the Ministry. Dr. Parks is a certified coach and an expert on emotional leadership, which is one of the key skill sets required for any civil servant. 

The Ministry would like to thank Dr. Parks for the very insightful and informative session.

2022-06-15
